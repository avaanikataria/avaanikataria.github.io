ABOUT - Girl's Golf

What is Berkeley High Girl's Golf team all about?

Practices and Games

We have practices on Monday and Wednesday. We usually have games on Tuesdays and Thursdays.

Timings and Location

The golf team practice at Tilden Golf Course from 4-6pm after school. We usually start try outs in the summer and start practicing as soon as possible because girl's golf season starts right away compared to other sports. Saying this, the golf season is only for 3 months. Therefore, one has to be very committed. When we have games we have to leave right after 6th period. Depending what time the games are at and how far we have to travel to have time to warm up. Sometimes we have to either leave half way through 5th period or at the end of 4th period in order to make it on time and have at least 30 minutes to warm-up
